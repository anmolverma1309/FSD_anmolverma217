Latest Project

Drop2Life – Smart India Hackathon Project

Problem Statement: India faces a severe water crisis due to rapid urbanization, depleting groundwater, and underused rainwater resources. Sustainable water management has become a pressing need in both urban and rural areas.

Solution Summary: Drop2Life is a data-driven, collaborative approach to promote rooftop rainwater harvesting and groundwater recharge. It brings together local communities, NGOs, and policymakers to foster sustainable, scalable solutions for water resilience.

Key Features:

  • Community-driven, data-informed planning
  • Engagement with NGOs, policymakers, and citizens
  • Scalable models for cities and towns
  • Continuous feedback and adaptability

Technologies & Tools Used:

  • GIS for mapping and analysis
  • IoT for water monitoring
  • Cloud-based data & analytics
  • Mobile & web platforms
  • Integration with government water initiatives

Societal & Industrial Impact: Empowers communities with real solutions for water security, encourages sustainable practices, supports jobs in water management, and sets a replicable model for urban and rural water conservation.

Role: Team Member – contributed to technical implementation, data analysis, and partnership building.
